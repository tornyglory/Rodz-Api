# Vehicle Service History — Frontend Brief

## What it is

The Vehicle Service History is a digital replacement for the physical service logbook that comes with a car. Instead of paper stamps and handwritten notes, every vehicle serviced at Rodz automatically builds a verified, kilometre-ordered history of everything that has ever been done to it — with photos, technician notes, costs, and links to the original invoices.

It is workshop-verified. The records are generated directly from the invoices raised by Rodz staff — not self-reported by the owner, not imported from a third-party source. Every entry is stamped with the workshop, the technician, the odometer reading, and the date. Photos taken during the job are attached to the relevant service entry.

---

## Why it matters

A physical service logbook is easy to fake, easy to lose, and tells the owner nothing useful beyond "it was serviced." Our digital version does far more:

- **For the vehicle owner:** A clear, always up-to-date record of every service and repair, viewable from their phone. They can see exactly what was done, at what mileage, what it cost, and see photos of worn parts before they were replaced.

- **For resale:** A buyer can instantly see the full verified service history — not a claim, but a documented record backed by workshop invoices and photos. A well-maintained car with a complete Rodz history is worth more.

- **For fleet operators:** A business running 50+ vehicles can see the complete maintenance record for every vehicle in their fleet. Compliance documentation, insurance claims, and scheduled maintenance become significantly easier.

- **For Rodz:** Every invoice raised automatically builds this history. There is no extra work for staff. The history is a by-product of doing business — and it becomes a compelling reason for customers to keep coming back to Rodz rather than going elsewhere (because switching means losing the history).

---

## How it works

### From the workshop side

When a staff member raises an invoice for a vehicle, the service history is updated automatically. There is nothing extra to do. The invoice line items, odometer reading, photos, technician, store, date, and cost are all captured at the time of invoicing and written to the vehicle's history log in real time.

When the invoice is sent to the customer, a link to the full invoice is attached to the relevant history entry. The customer can tap "View invoice" on any history entry to see the complete itemised receipt.

### From the owner's side

The vehicle history page is accessed by searching or navigating to a vehicle by registration plate. The page shows:

1. **Vehicle summary at the top** — make, model, year, registration, and current odometer. Beneath it, the total amount spent on maintenance across all recorded services.

2. **A vertical timeline** — each service visit is a card on the timeline, ordered from highest odometer to lowest (newest service at the top). The odometer reading is the primary axis — this makes it a true "life of the vehicle" view rather than just a date list.

3. **Each service card shows:**
   - The odometer at the time of service (e.g. "87,000 km")
   - The month and year of service (e.g. "Jun 2026")
   - The store and technician who did the work
   - An AI-generated plain English summary of what was done (e.g. *"Your vehicle received a full service including an oil and filter change. Rear brake pads were replaced on both sides — photos show significant wear before replacement."*)
   - A "View invoice →" link to the full public invoice, showing all line items, costs, and payment status
   - The total cost of that service visit
   - A horizontal photo strip showing all photos taken during that service — tap any photo to view it full screen

### AI summary

Each service entry includes a plain English summary generated by AI from the invoice line items and photos. This replaces the need to read through a list of part codes and labour items. The summary is written in plain language from the vehicle owner's perspective — clear, human, and informative.

If the AI summary hasn't been generated yet for an older entry, the raw line items are shown as a fallback.

## What it is not (Stage 1)

- It does not allow manual entries yet — all history comes from invoices raised in Rodz
- It does not include services performed at other workshops
- It is not a public page — it requires staff login to view

These are planned for future stages. The foundation built here supports all of them.

## API

**Base URL:** `https://fzzrkscwd7.execute-api.ap-southeast-2.amazonaws.com`

All routes require `Authorization: Bearer <accessToken>`.

### GET /vehicles/{rego}/service-history

```
GET /vehicles/ABC123/service-history
GET /vehicles/ABC123/service-history?limit=25&beforeOdometer=82500
Authorization: Bearer <accessToken>
```

**Query parameters:**

| Param | Type | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| `limit` | number | Page size. Default `25`, max `100`. |
| `beforeOdometer` | number | Pagination cursor — pass `nextCursor` from the previous response. |

### Field reference

| Field | Type | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| `vehicle.rego` | string | Registration plate |
| `vehicle.label` | string \| null | `"{year} {make} {model}"` |
| `vehicle.odometerCurrent` | number \| null | Latest recorded odometer in km |
| `lifetimeTotal` | number | Sum of all sent/paid invoice totals for this vehicle |
| `history[].invoiceId` | number | Use to deep-link to the internal invoice detail page |
| `history[].invoiceNumber` | string | Display reference e.g. `INV-2606-001` |
| `history[].invoiceUrl` | string \| null | Public customer invoice URL. Null if invoice hasn't been sent yet. Relative path — prepend your frontend base URL. |
| `history[].serviceDate` | string | `YYYY-MM-DD` |
| `history[].odometer` | number \| null | km at time of service. Null if not recorded on the invoice. |
| `history[].store` | string \| null | Store that performed the work |
| `history[].tech` | string \| null | Technician abbreviated name e.g. `"J. Smith"` |

| `401` | Not authenticated | Redirect to login |

## Implementation notes

- **Odometer formatting:** Always format as `87,000 km` with thousands separator. Never show the raw number.
- **`lifetimeTotal` excludes drafts** — only sent and paid invoices count.
- **Photos:** Use `<img src={urls.thumbnail}>` — never `fetch()` the URL. Cloudflare Images blocks browser cross-origin fetches.
- **`invoiceUrl` is relative** — prepend your frontend base URL to build the full link.
- **`aiSummary` fallback** — if null, render `items` as a bullet list of descriptions. Once AI generation is live, `aiSummary` will be populated on new invoices automatically.
- **Entries with no odometer** — group at the bottom under a "No odometer recorded" divider, sorted by date descending.
- **Pagination** — append results on "Load more" or infinite scroll. Pass `nextCursor` as `?beforeOdometer=` to fetch the next page. Stop when `hasMore` is false.
- **Staff deep-link** — use `invoiceId` to link staff to the internal invoice detail page at `/invoices/{invoiceId}`.
